An application typically deals with lots of things that have nothing to do with
the business logic or business process at hand. 
Examples:
  - Traffic management
  - Load Balancing
  - Specific input/output needs
  - AuthN/Z
Create a new layer to handle the activities listed above.
This is the API Gateway layer, also known as the orchestration layer.
